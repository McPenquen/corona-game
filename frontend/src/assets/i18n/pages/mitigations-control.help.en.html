Measures

Here you will find your main tool for managing a pandemic. In the game, you can also choose to compensate for some measures – this means that you will spend special resources to compensate damaged businesses.

In the game, for simplicity, the state is extremely effective in implementing and repealing measures. Everything is implemented almost immediately and always without technical errors. This is a major simplification compared to the real world. Remember that it takes some time for the measures to take effect on the number of people infected.

The measures slightly reduce the spread of the virus, but they also cost money and reduce stability in society. It's up to you what strategy you choose. We tried to make our model as fair as possible and allow for different approaches. For example, you can try to deploy and re-enable measures to avoid waves and hospital congestion.

Description of the measure

Veils, hands, spacing
a measure that is not costly and reduces the rate of spread of the infection
Closing borders
with the exception of transit traffic and a few other exceptions, cross-border traffic is restricted. The measure reduces the speed of spread, but negatively affects costs and social stability
Action Restrictions
measures limiting the maximum number of people gathered. It affects cultural or sporting events and various celebrations. It negatively affects the mood in society. The cancellation of events and tours is reflected in the cost of coping with a pandemic
Services
the closure of risky operations includes restaurants, bars, gyms and similar services where there is a high risk of transmission. The next stage is the closure of all shops and services, except the necessary ones (sale of food, fuels, etc.). Both measures reduce the rate of spread to some extent, but negatively affect costs and social stability.
Schools
choose which schools will be closed. Universities find it easier to switch to distance learning and the costs of closing them are lower. Primary schools are an important meeting place and transfer, but their closure has a strong negative impact on costs and social stability
No curfew
apart from shopping and the way to work, going out is fundamentally limited. It significantly negatively affects the mood in society, its maintenance is then very expensive
Extra compensation
the government compensates the already affected fields financially, this measure will significantly increase the compensation, the result will be less declining or even increasing stability
