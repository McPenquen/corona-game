Graphs

The main source of information on the current situation in the state. You can click between the following four graphs:

  • New infection This graph shows how many people became newly infected on a given day
  • Died Number of deaths per day. This chart shows people who died directly from covid-19. But keep in mind other circumstances. For example, congested hospitals without free capacity lead to more deaths.
  • Costs The cost graph shows the losses that the state treasury and the economy of the pandemic as a whole. Implementing measures, paying compensation and hospitalization - it all costs money.
  • Immunized This graph shows how people who underwent covid-19 and became immune for several months, and people who have acquired immunity by vaccination.

Graphs carry a lot of interesting information. But if you want to deal with the pandemic effectively, we recommend to orientate mainly according to the graph of newly infected, which reflects how well you are currently managing the pandemic.
